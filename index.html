Men Deserve Shelter Too

Welcome to the official advocacy and documentation repository challenging gender-based bias in homeless services.

This repository exists to give voice and visibility to men who have been excluded, displaced, or harmed by systemic discrimination under the guise of gender-based aid. It is a digital archive of stories, legal perspectives, and reform tools.
